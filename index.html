The Hunger Games Saga is a series written by Suzanne Collins. It is a dystopian fiction series that takes place in the country of Panem. The saga consists of the following books:

Core Series Books

  1. The Hunger Games (released in 2008)
  2. Catching Fire (released in 2009)
  3. Mockingjay (released in 2010)

Prequel Books

  1. The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes (released in 2020)
  2. Sunrise on the Reaping (released in 2025)
